Brown Butter Rice Krispie Treats

Description

The best rice krispies ever. The brown butter gives it a nice nutty flavor.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Box Family Size Rice Krispies
  • 2 12 Ounce Bags Regular Sized Marshmallows
  • 2 Sticks of Unsalted Butter

Steps

  1. Melt the butter over low flame until the butter browns and has a nutty aroma. About 5-10 minutes
  2. Once the butter is nicely browned add the marshmallows and a 1/2 teaspoon of table salt.
  3. Mix the marshmallows for about 5 minutes until they are melted and you can see the brown flecks throughout.
  4. Take off heat and pour in the rice krispies and mix to combine.
  5. Pour into a 9x13 baking dish that has been well oiled.
  6. Flatten and push into the sides and corners of the dish.
  7. Let cool slighly and then serve.
